## Production notes

- **First word — pick for the reaction.** Punchy option: **`GIG EM`** (Aggie flex, Longhorn groans — best reel payoff). Classic option: **`HELLO WORLD`** (the tech-audience trope; cleaner, less rivalry juice). Recommend GIG EM for a 90s reel.
- **Hook is everything.** Frame 1 needs the rivalry text on screen before anyone speaks — that's your scroll-stopper.
- **B-roll to grab while filming:** screw spinning loose, M3 threading in, print peeling off the bed, solenoids snapping into the 2×2, the diode close-up, the keys getting punched, the word appearing.
- **Audio:** upbeat build track that drops out right before the first-word clacks, then hits on the reveal. A trending sound works if it has a clean beat drop at ~1:08.
- **Caption idea:** "An Aggie and a Longhorn tried to build a robot that types. Week 3. 🤖⌨️ #engineering #3dprinting #arduino #txst… er, #gigem #hookem #robotics #diy #makersgonnamake"
